Our Expertise in Ponds

At Norcal Pond, we're dedicated to creating stunning water features that enhance the beauty of your outdoor space. Whether you're looking to install a new pond or need regular maintenance for an existing one, we've got you covered.

Design

Whatever the budget, we will work closely with you to design a custom pond that fits your preferences and complements your outdoor environment. From selecting the perfect location to choosing the right size and style, we ensure that your pond reflects your vision and adds tranquility to your space.

Construction

With years of experience in pond construction, we use quality materials and smart techniques to build gorgeous and durable water features. Our meticulous attention to detail ensures beauty and functionality that stands the test of time.

Maintenance

Keeping your pond in top condition is essential for its longevity and aesthetic appeal. Our maintenance services include water quality testing, debris removal, plant care, and equipment inspection. Trust us to keep your pond healthy and vibrant throughout the year.

Repairs

Is your pond in need of repairs or upgrades? We can diagnose and fix most issues to restore your pond to its former glory. Whether it's a leak, pump malfunction, or structural damage, we provide solutions to bring your pond back to life.
