Our Story

Norcal Pond started over 20 years ago as a small weekend project... a simple, backyard goldfish pool. Over two years time, that humble little pond grew into a sprawling passion project that spanned the length of the property, with four separate ponds, three streams, and six waterfalls. It was our own private oasis, complete with fish, frogs, water lilies, dragonflies, the works!

Soon, friends and neighbors were requesting ponds and water features in their own backyards. Now, with years of expertise and a passion for creating stunning aquatic landscapes, we bring your vision to life with precision and care. Whether you're looking for a small lily garden, a large koi pond, or a simple waterfall feature, we can make it happen.

At Norcal Pond, we love water gardens. We want your outdoor living space to be the very best it can be and take immense pride in maintaining the beauty and functionality of your water feature. Our maintenance services are designed to keep your aquatic oasis in top condition, promoting a healthy environment for your fish and plants. Trust us to provide the care and attention your pond deserves, so all you have to do is sit back and enjoy it.
